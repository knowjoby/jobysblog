## About This Blog

This blog is an automated daily resource for AI Product Managers at every stage of their career.

**What you'll find here:**
- Short, actionable articles (under 500 words) posted daily
- Structured roadmaps for beginner, advanced, and expert AI PMs
- Topics covering AI strategy, tooling, metrics, ethics, and more

**How it works:**
Articles are generated daily using a small language model (Microsoft Phi-3.5-mini) running via GitHub Actions — making this a fully automated, self-updating knowledge resource hosted entirely on GitHub.

**Who it's for:**
- Product Managers transitioning into AI roles
- PMs already working with AI/ML teams who want to level up
- Experienced leaders building AI product organizations
