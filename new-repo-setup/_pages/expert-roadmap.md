*For senior PMs and AI product leaders building at scale.*

---

## Stage 1: Design Your AI Product Organization (Quarter 1)

**Goal:** Build the team and processes that ship great AI products consistently.

- Define the PM–Data Science–Engineering triad model for your org
- Create an AI product review process (including ethics review gates)
- Build a shared evaluation framework teams can reuse across products
- Hire and develop PMs with AI product skills — what to look for

---

## Stage 2: Enterprise AI Strategy (Quarter 2)

**Goal:** Connect AI product decisions to business strategy and competitive positioning.

- Map your company's AI maturity: are you in exploration, scaling, or optimization?
- Identify AI initiatives that compound over time (data assets, model improvements, platform plays)
- Build the business case for AI infrastructure investment (GPUs, data pipelines, platform teams)
- Benchmark against competitors: where does AI create differentiation vs. parity?

---

## Stage 3: Scaling AI Products (Quarter 3)

**Goal:** Take AI from successful pilot to global scale.

- Architect for model serving at scale: latency, cost, availability trade-offs
- Manage multi-region AI deployments (data residency, model localization)
- Build self-healing AI systems: automated retraining pipelines, anomaly detection
- Design for graceful degradation: what happens when the model is down?

---

## Stage 4: AI Regulation and Governance (Quarter 4)

**Goal:** Lead responsibly in a fast-changing regulatory environment.

- Understand the EU AI Act risk tiers and what they mean for your products
- Build internal AI governance: model cards, audit trails, incident response
- Work with legal on IP issues (training data licensing, generated content ownership)
- Prepare for AI audits: documentation, explainability requirements

---

## Thought Leadership Areas
- AI product moats: network effects, proprietary data, model specialization
- The future of AI agents in products: agentic workflows, tool use, memory
- Build vs. buy in the foundation model era
- Measuring ROI on AI investment at the portfolio level
