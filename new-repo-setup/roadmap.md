## AI Product Manager Roadmaps

Choose your level to get a structured learning path:

---

### [Beginner Roadmap](/Ai-product-managers-blog/beginner-roadmap/)
*For PMs new to AI/ML*

- Understand AI/ML fundamentals
- Learn to work with data teams
- Ship your first AI feature
- Measure AI product success basics

---

### [Advanced Roadmap](/Ai-product-managers-blog/advanced-roadmap/)
*For PMs with 1–2 years of AI product experience*

- Build AI product strategy
- Master prompt engineering and LLMs
- Run A/B tests on AI features
- Lead responsible AI practices

---

### [Expert Roadmap](/Ai-product-managers-blog/expert-roadmap/)
*For senior PMs and AI product leaders*

- Design AI product organizations
- Make build vs. buy decisions for AI
- Scale AI products to millions of users
- Navigate AI regulation and compliance
