Short, operator-focused history of changes.

## 2026-03-12

- Branded the site as **Signal Log** (header + homepage copy).
- Homepage became the daily briefing + feed with search + quick filters.
- Fixed header layout so subtitle aligns cleanly with nav.
- `/now/` became an alias redirect to the homepage.
- Status + Archives became data-driven and operator-friendly.
- **Read more** replaced “Queue” as the public backlog label (deduped vs published).
- Archives gained search, tag filters, and pagination for large backlogs.
- Filters now persist locally (homepage + archives) and keyboard shortcuts were added (`/` focus search, `Esc` clear).
- Homepage marks clicked links as “read” (local-only) to help daily scanning.
- Generator hardened with DDG → RSS fallback.
